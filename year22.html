2022



JSCOP 4.0

(7th - 8th May 2022)

JIIT OSA STUDENT CHAPTER organises JSCOP for both technical as well as non-technical competitions to provide an ever-evolving platform for students from all nooks and corners. JIIT OSA conducts JSCOP every year as a ritual now that includes coding and programming events as well as hones the personality and professionalism of students with speaking and logical-thinking competitions. It also includes proper mentoring sessions and guidance that gives the students a wide platform to interact and widen their circle.

Crypto Beyond Trading

(9th July 2022)

We have all heard about NFTs and how they have become the fastest way to get "rich"! So to quench the queries regarding NFT's, DAO's and the use of Web 3.0 blockchain in Crypto Technology, we organised an interesting and highly informative webinar in association with CRYPTO CAPABLE with speaker as Mr. Gautam Padiyar, cofounder of Crypto Capable. During the course students learned about Blockchain Technology and how the world is applying it to their business models and changing dimensions in the metaverse.
